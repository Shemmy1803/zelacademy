Service related info

ZELDA COLLEGE is an institution that offers quality training with the help of well experienced lecturers.
Our School offers all programs as well as a two year schorlarship for students who can get at least 85% in our Zelda College Common entrance exam.
This exam is a kind of test and prep to know how much experience a student has in a course, and know what group of students to place them in, (We have three groups of students; The beginner, the intermediate and advanced students).

Not all students have experience in a course they're about to study, this exam is therefore voluntary.
In general, those who likely volunteer to take this exam are usually the advanced and intermediate students. Students who are well experienced in a course can be assigned as private tutors for beginners, and get Ten Dollars per student, depending on how well the tutor is able to impact knowledge into the beginner. In some cases, some student tutors make their beginner students take a minor exam based on what they've learnt within a whole month, and if their student passes by 85% above, we warrant them Thirty Dollars. Information on how teachers are hired and payed isn't included here for privacy reasons.
